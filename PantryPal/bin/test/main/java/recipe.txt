Chicken and Tomato Pancake Stack

Ingredients:

- 2 Tomatoes, diced
- 1/4 cup cooked Chicken, diced
- 1/2 cup Pancake Batter
- 2 tablespoons Maple Syrup

Instructions:

1. Preheat a non-stick pan over medium-high heat.

2. Pour the pancake batter into the pan and let it spread out.

3. Sprinkle the diced tomatoes and chicken over the pancake batter.

4. Cook the pancake for 4 minutes until the sides are lightly golden brown.

5. Use a spatula to flip the pancake and let it cook for an additional minutes.

6. Transfer the pancake to a serving plate and drizzle with maple syrup. Serve warm.
